Executive Summary

GitLab is adding usage-based AI monetization to a high-margin subscription model. Growth remains strong, but gross margin has stepped down, and the clearest visible pressure sits in the non-subscription bucket. Professional Services remains relevant inside that bucket, but it does not carry the company-level story on its own.

SectionContent
Main questionWhat does GitLab’s shift toward usage-based AI monetization imply for margin reading and performance interpretation?
Pressure pointWhat is happening inside the non-subscription bucket where revenue growth has lagged cost growth?
Scoped PS questionHow much weight should Professional Services carry inside that pressured bucket, and what still cannot be observed directly from public reporting?
